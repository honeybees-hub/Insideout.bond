About Currency Converter

The Insideout Currency Converter is a powerful financial tool designed to provide you with the most accurate and up-to-date exchange rates. Whether you're traveling, shopping internationally, or managing global investments, our tool ensures you have the data you need at your fingertips.

Key Features

  • Real-time exchange rates from reliable APIs.
  • Supports 34+ major global currencies.
  • Instant "Swap" functionality for easy reverse conversion.
  • Offline mode with 24-hour estimate fallback.

How to Use

  1. Enter the amount you wish to convert.
  2. Select the source currency (From).
  3. Select the target currency (To).
  4. View the converted result instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often are the rates updated?

Our exchange rates are refreshed multiple times a day via the ExchangeRate API to ensure high accuracy with global markets.

Is it safe to use?

Yes, all calculations are performed locally in your browser. We don't store your financial data or conversion history.

Which currencies are supported?

We support major currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, INR, CAD, AUD, and many more from around the world.
