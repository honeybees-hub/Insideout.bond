Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information (if any) is collected when you use Insideout.com, and how we handle it given that our website is entirely static and runs without a backend server.

1. Introduction

Insideout is designed with a "privacy-first" architecture. By operating as a static site, we minimize the data footprint generated during your visit. Most processing happens directly in your browser, keeping your data under your control.

2. Data We DO NOT Collect

No Personal Identifiers

We do not collect names, email addresses, or any unique identifier as we have no account system.

No Server-Side Content Storage

Data you process in our tools (e.g., text formatting) stays in your browser's memory. It is never sent to our servers.

No Server Logs

Since there is no application server, we do not generate usage logs related to the processing of your data.

3. Third-Party Services

Insideout embeds essential third-party services for advertising and analytics. These partners operate under their own privacy policies.

Advertising Networks

Partners use cookies to collect non-personal info (IP, browser type) to serve relevant ads. You can manage these in your browser settings.

Traffic Analytics

We use Google Analytics to measure anonymous traffic (pages viewed, device type). This data is aggregated and cannot identify you.

4. External Responsibility

When you follow a link from our results or ads to an external website, this policy no longer applies.

"We urge you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit after leaving Insideout. We are not responsible for their data practices."

5. Updates to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."
